Maculate

Maculate is the premier calculator app for macOS (iOS soon). It is designed to be simple, fast, and incredibly powerful.

Featuring

Hundreds of units and dozens of constants; unit & base conversions; solving for unknowns; factorization; calculus; matrices, vectors, complex numbers, and time; uncertainty propagation; interval arithmetic; some economics; statistics & combinatorics; variables; and much more!

Not Featuring

ads; tracking; data collection; in-app purchases; subscriptions; bloat.

Roadmap

Graphing, Currencies, iOS

With a little help from

Qalculate, GMP, and MPFR.
